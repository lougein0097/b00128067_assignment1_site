Airbnb

Many hosts put up their apartments, houses, studios etc on Airbnb for people visiting Istanbul. Although Airbnbs aren't very popular in Istanbul, there are still many to choose from. Prices of Airbnbs are very similar to prices of Hotels, so if you are looking for a vacation where you don't want to be disturbed and take care of yourself then an Airbnb is perfect.
