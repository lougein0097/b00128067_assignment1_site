Hotels

The city has a strong range of luxurious and upscale hotels and for people on a budget there are still beautiful hotels across the city with a local feel. If you want to sleep in something elegant and spacious, there are many luxury 5-star hotels including the Neorion Hotel, Best Western Empire Palace, Amber Hotel, and Byzantium Hotel that can match your standards with amenities including an onsite spa, restaurants, and stores. Those on a tight budget can indeed find cheaper hotels in Istanbul that have an awesome atmosphere as well as modern and basic amenities. These hotels, located in Istanbul's most romantic neighbourhoods, feel like like a house away from home.

Swissotel The Bosphorus Istanbul

Swissotel The Bosphorus Istanbul is a five-star premium hotel featuring a private rooftop pool and an award-winning spa. This hotel's excellent Bosphorus view puts it between walking distance of Nisantasi, the largest luxury shopping center. Prices range between the seasons but averages out at €530 a night.

CVK Park Bosphorus Hotel Istanbul

CVK Park Bosphorus Hotel Istanbul is located in the core of the capital, only 150 meters from Taksim Square. The hotel has trendy decor and two swimming pools, as well as a Spa and Fitness Centre, steam room,  Sauna and Turkish bath, massage services are also  available to guests. Prices range between the seasons but averages out at €630 a night.

The Marmara Taksim

The Marmara Hotel, which stands high above Istanbul's busy Taksim Square, promises luxury rooms with cable TV, free WiFi, and stunning views of the Bosphorus and the city. It has an enclosed swimming pool as well as a salon with a Turkish hammam. Prices range between the seasons but averages out at €178.50 a night, a cheaper option but still luxorious.

Radisson Blu Hotel Istanbul Ottomare

The Radisson Blu Hotel Istanbul Ottomare is situated in the Zeytinburnu region, only steps away from the port. An outdoor swimming pool and a lounge area are available at the hotel. There is also an enclosed pool and spa services on the premises. Prices range between the seasons but averages out at €136.25 a night, a cheaper option but still luxorious.

Hotel name

Legacy Ottoman lives up to its reputation, as it is located in a historical Byzantine-style building in Istanbul's centre, Eminönü. It has an enclosed pool with a hot tub, a hammam, a spa, and massage services. Guests can  relax in the sauna before jumping into the cold plunge pool at the hotel. Prices range between the seasons but averages out at €110 a night, a very cheaper option but an amazing option.

Crowne Plaza Istanbul - Old City, an IHG Hotel

The luxury Istanbul hotel is 400 meters from the Grand Bazaar. There is a spa, an indoor pool, and a gym on the premises. There is free private parking. Throughout the hotel, there is free WiFi. The 5-star Crowne Plaza Istanbul - Old City's glamorous rooms have satellite TV, a tea/coffee brewer, and a private bathroom with luxury toiletries. Prices range between the seasons but averages out at €81.25 a night, a very cheaper option but an amazing option.
