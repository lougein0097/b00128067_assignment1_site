The Beautiful City of Istanbul

Istanbul is a historical and modern global site of considerable significance. In the one side, the region is in one side Asia and the other region is in Europe. Istanbul is the most popular city in Turkey, as well as its financial and cultural base. Istanbul, situated on both sides of the Bosphorus, the small harbour between the Black Sea and the Marmara Sea, geographically and spiritually connects Asia and Europe. 

With its ancient mainland and many beautiful and historical attractions, Istanbul is a magnificently unique city that has become the capital of many civilizations from the beginning of history and is now home to people from all over the world. The population of Istanbul is expected to be between 12 and 19 million people, which is a huge number for a city as small as Istanbul, yet it is huge in people's hearts, as a result, it is one of the biggest cities in Europe and the world.
